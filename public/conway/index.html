What you are seeing

A Life-like cellular automaton is controlled by a B/S rule. A dead cell is born when its neighbor count is in the birth range, and a living cell survives when its neighbor count is in the survival range.

The line navigator shows nearby connected universes. Drag it to rotate the local neighborhood, then click a node to jump to that universe.

The Map button opens a space-like view of the larger connected multiverse.

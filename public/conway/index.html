What you are seeing

A Life-like cellular automaton is controlled by a B/S rule. A dead cell is born when its neighbor count is in the birth range, and a living cell survives when its neighbor count is in the survival range.

The orb shows nearby connected universes. Its lines now run directly through the universe nodes, so it acts more like a local four-dimensional compass.

The map is a zoomable live view of the larger connected multiverse. Drag it to pan, scroll to zoom, and click a tile to jump. When too many universes are visible, the map pauses those thumbnails and becomes an overview.
